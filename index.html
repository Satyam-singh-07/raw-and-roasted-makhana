Our Heritage

From Wetlands to Your Home

Makhana harvesting is an art form. Our farmers dive into freshwater ponds to collect the seeds of the Euryale ferox plant. These seeds are then sun-dried, roasted over high heat, and hand-popped to create the white kernels you love.

By choosing Raw & Roasted, you're supporting sustainable agriculture and centuries-old traditional techniques.
