Our Heritage & Story

Preserving the ancient craft of Mithila makhana for the modern wellness journey.

Rooted in Purity

Founded with a vision to bring the "White Gold" of Mithila to the global stage, Raw & Roasted Makhana is more than just a brand. It's a bridge between ancient agricultural wisdom and contemporary lifestyle.

Our journey begins in the pristine wetlands where lotus seeds are hand-harvested by local communities who have perfected this art over generations. We ensure that every kernel we pack reflects the purity of its origin.

What We Stand For

Ethical Sourcing

We work directly with farmers, ensuring fair wages and supporting local rural economies in Bihar.

100% Natural

No chemicals, no preservatives. Our roasting process uses traditional methods to keep nutrition intact.

Sustainability

From water-neutral harvesting to eco-friendly packaging, we care for the earth that feeds us.
