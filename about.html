About Poppinuts

Poppinuts is a premium healthy snacking brand made from air-popped water lily seeds, also known globally as lotus seeds or makhana. These naturally popped seeds are celebrated for their high protein content, light texture, and clean nutritional profile, making them a true superfood.

We believe healthy food should be simple, clean, and naturally powerful. Our seeds are carefully sourced, air-popped without oil, and rigorously quality-checked to ensure every pop delivers freshness, crunch, and nourishment.

Whether enjoyed roasted, flavored, or used as a cooking ingredient, Poppinuts supports a balanced, protein-rich lifestyle with every serving.

About Shree Lotus Exports Pvt. Ltd.

Shree Lotus Exports Pvt. Ltd. is a trusted Indian company specializing in sourcing, processing, and exporting high-quality agro and food products to both domestic and international markets.

With a strong focus on ethical sourcing, quality assurance, and global food safety standards, our operations blend traditional knowledge with modern processing excellence.

Poppinuts is our flagship healthy snacking brand, representing our commitment to superfood nutrition, purity, and premium quality.
